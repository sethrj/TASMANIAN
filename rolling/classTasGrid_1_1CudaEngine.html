Encompassing dense matrix-matrix or matrix-vector multiplication.

The raw operation is where A is M by K, B is K by N, and C is M by N. The signature is almost identical to BLAS dgemm() and Nvidia cublasDgemm(). Handles special cases when some of the dimensions are 1, then matrix-vector functions will be called. Automatically calls CUDA or MAGMA libraries at the back-end.

Assumes that all vectors have the correct order.

Encompassing sparse matrix-matrix or matrix-vector multiplication.

The raw operation is where A is M by K, B is K by N, and C is M by N. The matrix B is stored in compressed column format, transpose version cusparseDcsrmm2(). Handles special cases when some of the dimensions are 1, then matrix-vector functions will be called. Automatically calls CUDA or MAGMA libraries at the back-end.

Assumes that all vectors have the correct size.
