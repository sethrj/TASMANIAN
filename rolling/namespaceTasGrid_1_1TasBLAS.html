Wrapper for BLAS dense matrix-matrix and matrix-vector.

Common API computing where A is M by K, B is K by N, and C is M by N. Transposes are not considered (not needed by Tasmanian). The method switches between dgemm_ and dgemv_ depending on the appropriate dimensions.
